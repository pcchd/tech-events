Tech Events in Chandigarh

  • January 14, 2016 Web Scraping for fun and profit

    In this talk Bineet will discuss the chronicles of web scraping, and how it can prove useful in events where some web apps/sites don't provide APIs (or limit their APIs too much to be useful). He will also discuss the challenges he and his team face at their scraper heavy startup SpiderOrb. Attendees will get to see the promised land where you can apply any structure of your choice to the chaos of the Internet.

    Starts at: 10:00 am Duration: 2 hours Location: Sector 37 RSVP
